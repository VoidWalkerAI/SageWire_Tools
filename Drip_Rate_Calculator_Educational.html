Drip Rate Calculator (Educational Use Only)

This tool does ONLY the drip math: converts volume, time, and drop factor into gtt/min, and shows the equivalent IV pump rate in mL/hr.

Safety warning: This is NOT a medical device, NOT a prescribing tool, and NOT a replacement for hospital pumps, protocols, or pharmacy. Always use your institution’s approved calculators and devices for real patients.
