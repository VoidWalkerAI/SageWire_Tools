Concrete Volume – Slab Helper

Estimate concrete volume for flat slabs (floors, pads, driveways). Enter dimensions and get cubic yards plus bag counts.

Concrete Volume – Footing / Foundation Strip

Estimate concrete for continuous footings or strip foundations. Treat it like a long rectangular trench.

Bag counts use approximate yields:
• 80 lb bag ≈ 0.60 ft³  • 60 lb bag ≈ 0.45 ft³.
Always add a waste factor in real projects (spillage, uneven forms, etc.). Structural design (rebar, frost depth, code rules) is beyond this helper.
