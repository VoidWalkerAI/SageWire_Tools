Tap Drill Size Calculator

Approximate tap drill size from thread data. For inch threads (60° UN/UNC/UNF): drill ≈ major diameter − (1 ÷ TPI) ≈ 75% thread. For metric: drill ≈ major diameter − pitch. Always confirm with tool charts and manufacturer data for critical work.
