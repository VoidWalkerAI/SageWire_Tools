Dosage Calculator (Educational Use Only)

This tool does ONLY the math for weight-based dosing: converts patient weight and an ordered dose in mg/kg into total mg and mL based on a concentration (mg/mL).

Safety warning: This is NOT a medical device, NOT a prescribing tool, and NOT a replacement for hospital calculators, protocols, pharmacy, or clinical judgment. Always follow your institution’s procedures and double-check every dose with approved tools.
